=== MEDICATIONS ===

Paracetamol (Acetaminophen)
- Dose: 500-1000mg every 4-6 hours
- Max: 4000mg per day
- Use: Pain relief, fever
- Warning: Avoid alcohol, can cause liver damage

Ibuprofen
- Dose: 200-400mg every 6-8 hours  
- Max: 1200mg per day (OTC)
- Use: Pain, inflammation, fever
- Warning: Take with food, avoid if stomach ulcers

=== FIRST AID ===

CPR (Adult)
1. Check responsiveness
2. Call emergency (112/108)
3. 30 chest compressions (5-6cm deep, 100-120/min)
4. 2 rescue breaths
5. Repeat 30:2 until help arrives

Burns
- Cool under water for 20 minutes
- Do NOT use ice or butter
- Cover with clean cloth
- Seek medical help if large area

=== CHRONIC CONDITIONS ===

Type 2 Diabetes
- Symptoms: Increased thirst, frequent urination, fatigue
- Management: Diet, exercise, medication (Metformin)
- Target HbA1c: <7%
- Monitor: Blood sugar, annual eye/kidney checks

Hypertension
- Target: <130/80 mmHg
- Lifestyle: Low sodium, exercise, weight loss
- Medications: ACE inhibitors, ARBs, diuretics
- Monitor: Check BP regularly

=== EMERGENCY SIGNS ===

Seek immediate help if:
- Chest pain with sweating, nausea
- Sudden severe headache ("worst ever")
- Difficulty breathing
- Uncontrolled bleeding
- Loss of consciousness
- Stroke signs: Face drooping, arm weakness, speech difficulty

Emergency Numbers:
National: 112
Ambulance: 108
